About K(ompetenzp)ool

Kool is a Software engeneering project done by six students of KIT. It was developed at AIFB in the scope of the "Teamprojekt Softwareentwicklung" and is part of the Kompetenzpool project.
Only a limited number of computers were harmed during development.

You can search for a desired competency and are shown researchers who posses this competency based on their publications The competencies are extracted from a database of publications using a NLP machine learning model. Therefore neither their quality nor their accuracy can be guaranteed.
