About K(ompetenzp)ool

Kool is a Software engineering project developed by students at the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology. It was developed at AIFB in the context of the "Teamprojekt Softwareentwicklung". The project is part of the Kompetenzpool project.
Only a limited number of computers were harmed during development.

The tool enables you to search for researchers based on their competencies. Competencies are assigned to the researchers based on their publications at the KIT. They are extracted from a database of publications using a NLP machine learning model (see details in the project wiki). Neither their quality nor their accuracy can be guaranteed, especially on out of context data (i.e. abstracts) that were not used in the development of the project.
